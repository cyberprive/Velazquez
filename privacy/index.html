2. Data we process

Depending on how you interact with Real Zero, we may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact data — name, email, phone, company, message content (when you submit a form or contact us by email or WhatsApp).
  • Payment data — when you purchase a product at a Real Zero recovery station, payment is processed by our payments processor (see Section 3). Real Zero does not store full card numbers or sensitive cardholder data; we receive only the transaction reference, amount, masked PAN, timestamp, and authorisation result needed for accounting, refunds and incident management.
  • Station-usage data — anonymised or pseudonymised operational data from the recovery stations (product selected, time of service, station identifier) used for service quality, hygiene traceability, and reporting.
  • Website-usage data — privacy-respecting analytics that do not identify individual users. See our cookie policy.

Nayax — Payment Processing

When you pay at a Real Zero recovery station, the card or mobile-wallet transaction is processed by Nayax Ltd. acting as a processor on Real Zero's behalf. Nayax also acts as an independent controller for certain technical and anti-fraud purposes related to the payment service. You can read Nayax's privacy policy at https://www.nayax.com/legal-and-privacy/privacy-policy/.

4. Purposes and legal basis

  • Responding to enquiries — consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR) when you submit a form, or pre-contractual measures (Art. 6.1.b GDPR) when your enquiry relates to a potential commercial relationship.
  • Processing your payment at a station — performance of the contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR) between you and Real Zero for the purchase of the product.
  • Accounting, tax and refund obligations — legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c GDPR).
  • Service quality, hygiene traceability and fraud prevention — legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR), balanced against your rights and freedoms.

5. Retention

  • Enquiry data not converted into a commercial relationship: max 12 months from last contact.
  • Transaction records: retained for the periods required by Spanish tax and commercial law (up to 6 years).
  • Station support data (form submissions from realzero.es/velazquez): 24 months from submission.

6. International transfers

Some processors are located outside the European Economic Area. These transfers are covered by the Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and, where applicable, by supplementary measures designed to guarantee a level of protection essentially equivalent to that of the GDPR.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, object to, restrict the processing of, and to data portability over your personal data. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacidad@realzero.es attaching a copy of an identity document. We will respond within one month.

If you believe that the processing of your personal data does not comply with applicable law, you may lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) or your local EU data protection authority.

8. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit (HTTPS), least-privilege access controls, and PCI-DSS compliance through our payments processor. Real Zero does not store full cardholder data on its own systems.

9. Changes to this Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The date of last update appears at the top. Material changes will be communicated through this website.
