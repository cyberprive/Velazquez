The product

Zero Touch 2.

Automated shake-preparation machine designed to live inside the studio. Matte black body, touchscreen UI, contactless payment, remote telemetry. Each shake is freshly prepared in roughly 30 seconds.

Specs

Designed for the premium studio.

Dimensions

60 × 80 × 180 cm (depth × width × height). Fits in any reception or wide corridor without occupying class space.

Time per shake

Each shake is prepared fresh in roughly 30 seconds. Designed to slot into the post-class flow without interrupting it.

Connectivity

Wi-Fi 5 GHz + 4G backup. Real-time telemetry: stock, temperatures, sales, alerts.

Payment

Contactless payment. Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay. Authorised payment operator, PCI-DSS compliant.

Cleaning

Automatic self-cleaning cycle after every serving. On-site deep cleaning by our team outside opening hours.

Energy

Standard 230 V / 16 A outlet. Average consumption 0.3 kWh/day. Low-power mode during off-hours.

What's inside

Clean protein. Clear choices.

Base protein

Several flavours available from launch, rotated based on studio feedback. 25 g+ protein per serving. Zero or ~1 % carbs in most references — hence the name Real Zero.

Add-on

Creatine (5 g per serving) as an optional add-on. On-screen selection with transparent price modifier. More add-ons under evaluation.

Suppliers

Recognised European and US protein brands, with documented technical sheets and quality controls. Allergen information visible before each purchase.

Hygiene and compliance

European food-safety standards.

The machine complies with European regulations applicable to automated food machines. Food-grade materials, continuous temperature sensors, per-shake batch traceability. Monthly internal audit.

Self-cleaning

Scheduled cycles using hot water and food-grade detergent. The studio needs no dedicated staff.

Traceability

Every serving is logged with batch, temperature and timestamp. In case of incident, full info within seconds.

Food information

Ingredients and allergens visible before confirming purchase. Compliance with EU Regulation 1169/2011.

Waste management

Recyclable cups. We work with the studio to align the collection point with their existing recycling flow.

Install requirements

All we need from the studio.

  • → A flat surface of at least 70 × 90 cm near the exit or changing rooms.
  • → 230 V / 16 A outlet within 2 m.
  • → Stable Wi-Fi coverage (SSID and password provided at install).
  • → Loading/unloading access (the machine weighs 140 kg, mounted on hydraulic cart).
  • → Install window of 2–4 hours, ideally outside peak hours.

FAQ

Frequent questions.

Who handles protein restocking?

Our team visits each machine several times a week — typically every other day, or as triggered by telemetry — to restock, check hygiene and test operation. The studio doesn't manage inventory.

What if the machine fails?

Telemetry detects incidents in real time and our team is alerted automatically. SLA: 24 hours for critical issues, 72 hours for minor issues. First 14 days after install: 24/7 support.

Can we customise the vinyl?

Yes. The machine is always installed with a co-branded vinyl respecting your studio's identity. We design the graphic proposal during prep, with your marketing team.

How many shakes a day do we need?

We consider any install above 12–18 shakes per day operationally sustainable. If after 90 days we don't reach that volume, we remove the machine at no cost to the studio.

And deep cleaning?

The machine runs an automatic self-cleaning cycle after every serving. On top of that, our team performs on-site deep cleaning outside studio opening hours, using certified food-grade products. Cleaning logs available on the dashboard.

What about allergies?

The screen displays ingredients and allergens before purchase. The shake contains dairy protein (whey) and a vegan option will always be available. We cannot guarantee the absence of cross-contamination inside the machine, so anyone with a diagnosed allergy to lactose or milk protein should not use it.

Can members pay with gift cards or studio membership?

Yes, gift cards are available from launch. Day-to-day payment is direct contactless, and we're working on integration with membership-management systems (Mindbody, ClubWare and similar) to enable member-account charges or pre-payment tied to their fee. Planned for Q4 2026.
