Real case · Pilot

Orangetheory Fitness Velázquez, Madrid.

Real Zero's first installation. Active since January 2026. Integrated with the coaches, operated by our team, validated with real members.

The problem

Members train hard and leave hungry.

OTF Velázquez has some of Madrid's most consistent members. They finish each class empty, with the optimal recovery window open, and nowhere nearby to close it. The coach would recommend protein, but that recommendation died at the studio door.

The install

Two-week fit-out. Zero disruption.

Week 1: technical visit, measurements, approval of the co-branded vinyl with OTF Spain (approved by Marketing Director Melanie Wolk).

Week 2: overnight install after closing, internal testing with the team, training with the 5 coaches (Alejo, Antonio, Bea, Jorge, Paco) — each associated with a flavour or add-on as "their" recommendation.

Day 1 operational: January 2026. Internal announcement coordinated with OTF Spain team.

What we've learned

Preliminary pilot results.

Daily adoption

Sustained growth from week one. Daily volume above the operational break-even threshold.

*Detailed figures available under NDA.

Repeat rate

More than half of members who tried the product after their first class bought again within the next 7 days.

Coach attribution

70% of first purchases follow an explicit coach recommendation at class end. Validation of the coach-led conversion model.
