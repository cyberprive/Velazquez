Pre-launch tasting

Testing flavours with the coaches.

In April 2026 we ran the first tasting session with the studio coaches to validate flavours, dosing and texture. Their direct feedback defined the two opening references available from launch day.

Tasting session · Orangetheory Fitness Velázquez · April 2026

The problem

Members train hard and leave hungry.

OTF Velázquez has some of Madrid's most consistent members. They finish each class empty, with the optimal recovery window open, and nowhere nearby to close it. The coach recommends protein, but that recommendation dies at the studio door. Real Zero closes the gap.

Launch plan

From install to go-live in two weeks.

What we will measure

The pilot is also a measurement system.

Daily adoption

Daily volume tracked from day one. Operational threshold defined at 12–18 shakes per day.

Repeat rate

Percentage of members who buy again within 7 days of their first purchase. Key indicator that the ritual has taken hold.

Per-coach attribution

Traceability of which coach brings in each first-time buyer. Real-time validation of the coach-led conversion model.
