The company

We build what the studio was missing.

Real Zero is a Spanish company that operates automated post-workout recovery infrastructure. We're not a supplement brand. We're not a vending company. We install and operate fresh-shake machines inside premium fitness studios.

Founder

Daniel Escobar.

Founder and operator of Real Zero. Background in technology and retail operations. Has been training at Orangetheory Velázquez for more than a year — started as personal rediscovery, ended as a front-row seat to a real problem no one was solving.

The idea didn't come from a market study. It came from rushing out of class to mix a decent shake at home, watching other members do the same, and realising the post-workout nutrition recommendation was dying at the door for many members in a hurry. The space to solve it exists. The product doesn't.

Real Zero was built to close that loop. Turn the moment class ends into the moment recovery starts — no friction, no walking, no thinking. A complete ritual before you leave the gym.

What we are · What we're not

Clarity from day one.

Principles

Four things that matter to us.

Operational simplicity

The studio shouldn't notice the machine. No coach should spend time on us. Everything we do reduces friction, not adds it.

Transparency

Ingredients in plain sight. Open reports. A direct WhatsApp line with the founder. If something breaks, we know before the studio asks.

Respect for the studio brand

The studio is the protagonist. Our machine integrates into its visual identity. We never compete with the member's experience.

European pace

We don't grow at any cost. We grow at the speed we can deliver a premium service. Every installed machine is a long-term commitment.

Team

A small team. Focused.

Real Zero currently operates with a small team led by the founder and external collaborators for field operations (restocking, cleaning), graphic design and logistics. We're growing the team at the pace of active installations, not before.

If you're interested in joining the team in operations, design or partnerships, write to us at hola@realzero.es.
