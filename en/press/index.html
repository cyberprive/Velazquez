Corporate description.

Short version (40 words):

Real Zero is a Spanish company that installs and operates automated protein-shake machines inside premium fitness studios. Founded in Madrid by Daniel Escobar, it has been operating its first pilot at Orangetheory Fitness Velázquez since January 2026.

Long version (150 words):

Real Zero S.L. is a Spanish sport-nutrition infrastructure company. Its flagship product is Zero Touch 2, an automated machine that serves fresh protein shakes in 30 seconds, designed to be installed inside boutique and premium fitness studios. The business model is revenue share: the machine is installed at no cost to the gym, which receives a percentage of every shake sold. Real Zero manages everything: hardware, consumables, restocking, cleaning, payments and support. Founded in 2026 by Daniel Escobar, it's headquartered in Madrid and currently operates a pilot at Orangetheory Fitness Velázquez. The thesis: post-workout recovery must happen inside the studio, not at the door.
