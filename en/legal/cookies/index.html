Cookie policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file that a website downloads onto your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit. They are commonly used to remember preferences, measure site usage or personalise content.

2. Cookies on this site

This site has been designed around the principle of data minimisation. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies or social-network cookies. Therefore, we do not display a cookie consent banner, since we do not process personal data through non-essential cookies.

Technical cookies (exempt from consent)

We may use strictly necessary cookies for the service to function. These are exempt from the duty to inform and obtain consent under Art. 22.2 of the Spanish LSSI-CE and the AEPD guidelines:

CookieOwnerPurposeDuration
rz-lang-seen Real Zero (sessionStorage) Avoid redirecting the user to the auto language more than once per session Session

Note: rz-lang-seen is technically a sessionStorage value (not an HTTP cookie), but we declare it here for transparency.

3. Analytics

To understand aggregate site usage (most-visited pages, traffic source, devices) we may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not identify individual users (for example, Vercel Analytics in cookieless mode or Plausible Analytics).

These tools process exclusively aggregated and anonymised data and therefore do not require consent under the European Data Protection Board (EDPB) or AEPD guidelines.

4. Third-party cookies

When you click a link to an external service (WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn, email), you leave this site and enter the third-party platform, whose cookie and privacy policies are that operator's responsibility. We recommend you review them directly:

  • WhatsApp cookie policy
  • Instagram (Meta) cookie policy
  • LinkedIn cookie policy

5. How to block or delete cookies

Although this site does not use cookies that require consent, you can configure your browser to block or delete any cookie. Each browser offers specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to this policy

If in the future we add analytics or advertising cookies, we will implement a prior consent system (cookie banner) in accordance with applicable regulations (GDPR, LSSI-CE, 2023 AEPD guidelines). Any material change will be reflected on this page and, where appropriate, we will request your consent.

7. Contact

For any enquiries about cookie usage on this site, email us at privacidad@realzero.es.
