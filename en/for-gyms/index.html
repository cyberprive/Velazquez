Model

What the install includes

Turnkey. No exceptions.

Hardware and consumables

Zero Touch 2 machine, co-branded vinyl matching your studio's identity, branded cups, initial protein stock, first cleaning and spare-parts kit.

Coach integration

Training session with your coaches, optional per-coach flavour or add-on assignment ("your coach's pick"), monthly adoption report by recommender.

Remote operation

Real-time telemetry, stock alerts, contactless payments via authorised operator, restocking and on-site checks handled several times a week by our team.

Monthly reports

Dashboard with daily sales, peak hours, repeat rate, per-coach attribution, average ticket. Actionable data for studio marketing.

Who it's for

Built for premium studios.

We work best in facilities with the following characteristics:

  • → Boutique or premium, with monthly fee above the local market median.
  • → 300+ active members with regular attendance.
  • → Traffic concentrated in classes or guided sessions (HIIT, indoor, reformer pilates, CrossFit box, personal training).
  • → Showers in the changing rooms — signals real post-workout culture.
  • → Coaches present at class end, available for personal recommendations.
  • → Reception with space for a 60 × 80 cm machine near the exit.
